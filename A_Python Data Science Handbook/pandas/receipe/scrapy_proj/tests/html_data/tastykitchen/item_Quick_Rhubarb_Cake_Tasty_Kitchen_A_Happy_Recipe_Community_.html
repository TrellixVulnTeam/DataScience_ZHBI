Quick Rhubarb Cake

by theseasidebaker on April 7, 2013 in Cakes, Desserts

See this recipe post on theseasidebaker’s site!

0.00 Mitt(s) 0 Rating(s)

Prep Time 15 Minutes

Cook Time 30 Minutes

Difficulty Easy

Updated

Share   

8
  • + Print Recipe

Recipe Description

Spring has sprung, and so have our rhubarb plants! Make this quick and easy cake to show off your garden harvest!

Preparation Instructions

Preheat oven to 350ºF.

In a bowl mix eggs and 2/3 cup of sugar until light. Add melted butter, vanilla, and buttermilk. Stir in flour, baking powder and salt and mix to combine.

Pour into an 8 or 9-inch cake pan that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Spread diced rhubarb around the top of the batter and sprinkle with remaining 2 tablespoons sugar.

Bake at 350ºF for 30 minutes or until an inserted knife comes out clean.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole Eggs
  • ⅔ cups Sugar Plus 2 Tablespoons, Divided
  • 3 Tablespoons Melted Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Or Vanilla Bean Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons Buttermilk
  • 1 cup Plus 2 Tablespoons, All-purpose Flour
  • ¾ teaspoons Baking Powder
  • ⅛ teaspoons Salt
  • 2 cups Diced Rhubarb
