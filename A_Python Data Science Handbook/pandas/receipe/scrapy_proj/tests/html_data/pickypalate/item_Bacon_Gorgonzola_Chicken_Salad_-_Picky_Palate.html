Bacon Gorgonzola Chicken Salad

Tags: bacon, blue cheese dressing, celery, chicken, gorgonzola, mayonnaise, salad, snack, superbowl



Chicken Salad is such a great recipe to serve at parties.  You can serve it with some great crackers or inside a fabulous soft roll and everyone seems to be happy!  Wait until you try my flavor packed Bacon Gorgonzola Chicken Salad.  It has crispy bacon pieces gorgonzola cheese throughout.

I love that you just toss all of your ingredients in one bowl, stir and you are ready enjoy.  Perfect for game day munching or lunches during the week.  Enjoy!

Let’s start with some cooked shredded chicken breast.  You can either use a rotisserie chicken and shred it or bake your own and shred it.  I like to place 2 chicken breasts into a baking pan with 1/4 cup chicken broth, cover lightly with foil and bake for 50-60 minutes until cooked through.  Let rest for 15 minutes before shredding.

 

Finely chop 2 small stalks of celery.  You’ll add bacon slices, Gorgonzola, mayonnaise and blue cheese dressing.  Click HERE to see a 6 second video from Vine of the salad getting put together

1 tablespoon finely chopped thyme leaves.

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice.

Ahhh, stir all together and get ready to enjoy!

Can’t you practically taste the bacon pieces inside?!

Bacon Gorgonzola Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked shredded chicken breast
  • 8 strips cooked crumbled bacon
  • 1/4 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
  • 3/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup prepared blue cheese dressing
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into a large bowl. Stir to combine. Taste and season with additional seasonings if needed. Serve with firm crackers or serve inside a soft roll.

Makes 8 Servings
